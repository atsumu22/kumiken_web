About Us

Over 30 years of experience and achievements.

Kumi Ken Co.,Ltd have taken an important role in F&B industry, since it was established in 1990.

As the food culture gets diversified, we have continued to make our clients’ wish come true regardless of genre, such as Japanese restaurants, Korean restaurants, food factories and supermarket backyard kitchens.

We are specialists dealing with commercial kitchen equipment such as refrigerators, hydro-vent hood system, auto-noodle boiler, convection oven and so on.

Nowaday, automation of kitchens is getting important. Following the trend, we have worked on in the field of automated Food Delivery System, especially “Bullet Train Delivery System (Sushi Train)” and “Sushi Conveyor Belt”.

We are willing to keep learning.

All for your success.
