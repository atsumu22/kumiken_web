About Us

In 1990, being one of the pioneer in the commercial kitchen solutions history, Kumi Ken COMPANY LIMITED was established.
Kumi Ken provides custom made stainless steel appliances such as Hydro-Vent Hood System, S.S. stoves, S.S. cabinets, S.S. tables and S.S sinks etc.
Design, supply and install for restaurants, catering services business with wide range of quality equitments.
Since, Kumi Ken Co. Ltd. was focused on the Stainless Steel products for Chinese Restaurants Projects in Hong Kong and China.
To catch with the faster growth in these areas, and Commercial Refrigerators, Hydro-Vent Hood System, Auto-noodel boiler, Oven etc.were needed.
KumiKen extends the business scopes to Janpanese Restaurants, Korean Restaurants and Chain-Supermarket.
Nowaday, Following the trend of F&B industry, Automated Food Delivery System, especially “Bullet Train Delivery System (Sushi Train)” and “Sushi Conveyor Belt”.
We provide a "One-stop" Solution for all architecture and kitchen design firms.
